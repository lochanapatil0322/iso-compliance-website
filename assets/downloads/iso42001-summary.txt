ISO 42001:2023 Summary Notes

ISO 42001 is related to AI management systems. It helps organizations manage risks connected to developing, providing, or using artificial intelligence systems.

Key AI governance areas:
- AI policy
- AI system inventory
- AI risk assessment
- Data governance
- Human oversight
- Transparency
- Accountability
- Monitoring and improvement
- AI incident handling

Common AI risks:
- Bias and unfair outcomes
- Privacy issues
- Lack of explainability
- Security attacks on AI systems
- Incorrect or harmful AI output
- Poor accountability

Simple explanation:
ISO 42001 helps organizations manage AI responsibly by creating policies, controls, monitoring, and accountability around AI systems.

Educational note:
These notes are original summaries for learning purposes and are not a replacement for the official ISO standard.
