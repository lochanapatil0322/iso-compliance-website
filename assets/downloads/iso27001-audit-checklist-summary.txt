ISO 27001 Audit Checklist Summary Notes

An ISO 27001 audit checklist helps auditors verify whether an organization's Information Security Management System is planned, implemented, maintained, and improved.

Common audit areas:
- ISMS scope
- Leadership commitment
- Information security policy
- Risk assessment method
- Risk treatment plan
- Asset ownership
- Access control
- Security awareness
- Incident response
- Backup and recovery
- Internal audits
- Corrective actions
- Management review

Example audit questions:
- Is the ISMS scope clearly documented?
- Are information security risks identified and reviewed?
- Are access rights approved and reviewed?
- Are security incidents recorded and investigated?
- Are audit findings tracked until closure?

Simple explanation:
The checklist converts ISO 27001 ideas into practical questions that help auditors collect evidence.

Educational note:
These notes are original summaries for learning purposes and are not a replacement for the official ISO standard.
